One subject can have 2 or more verbs. In this case, we call the pattern a trunk with 2 parts, or T= We pronounce this "T equals." Examples: Sometimes a tree trunk divides near the base and grows into 2 separate parts. 1 Subject: A tree trunk 2 verbs: divides and grows This tree is in a forest and has lots of room to grow. 1 Subject: This tree 2 verbs: is and has
