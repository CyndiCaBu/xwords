Transcendence is a little hard to explain. Some definitions include the idea of going beyond the ordinary world, beyond regular limits. Transcendence can refer to excellence. It can refer to a spiritual or philosophical sense of being free from our physical limitations, beyond this material world. It might be difficult to understand if you haven’t actually experienced at least some sense of it.

Fortunately, to understand the idea of transcendence in positive psychology, we can focus on the character strengths related to it. These are strengths that help us make connections to the larger universe and find meaning in our lives. You can learn more about each of them by clicking them below.
