All over the world, people have specific ideas about what&#8216;s right and what&#8216;s wrong. This is a sense of morals, beliefs about what we should and shouldn&#8217;t do. It includes ideas about moral excellence, things we should strive for. Examples of moral excellence are called virtues.
In almost every culture and tradition, there are certain virtues that people value. These virtues help us live good lives, full of meaning. They&#8216;re essential for thriving. In positive psychology, cultures with a written history showing their religions and traditions were studied to find virtues that have had an important influence for thousands of years. This means texts were analyzed from &#8220;Confucianism and Taoism in China, Buddhism and Hinduism in South Asia, and ancient Greece, Judeo-Christianity and Islam in the West,&#8221; according to the 2004 book Character Strengths and Virtues.
